### Masa (Hindu Calendar App) Privacy Policy

---

**Privacy Policy**

This privacy policy applies to the Masa app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Ramki Pitchala & Rohan Shiva (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

---

**Information Collection and Use**

The Application is designed to run entirely on your device. It does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data to any external servers or third parties.

The only permission the Application requests is access to your **device's location**, which is used solely for calculating Panchanga attributes such as Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana, Vaara, and festival timings — all of which depend on your geographic position.

- **Your location data never leaves your device.**
- It is used only for calendar-related calculations.
- No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected or stored.
- The app does **not** use any analytics, ads, or third-party tracking.

---

**Third Party Access**

The Application does **not** share any user data with third parties. Since no data is collected or stored, there is nothing to share or transmit externally.

---

**Opt-Out Rights**

You can stop all use of the Application by simply uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstall processes available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace.

---

**Data Retention Policy**

The Application does not collect or retain any user data. Since there is no user data stored, there is nothing to delete or manage.

---

**Children**

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The Application is safe for all age groups and does not solicit or process any data from users, including children.

---

**Security**

The Application runs entirely on the user’s device and does not transmit any data. There is no remote data storage, reducing the risk of data leaks or unauthorized access.
